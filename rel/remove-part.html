Remove a part

Parts can be easily removed if they are not needed. Some parts, namely the title and the body of the topic, are required and cannot be removed.

  1. Find the part in the Guidance panel.
  2. Remove any existing content in that part.
    If you see a checkmark next to the part name, then you have content in that part that must be removed before you can remove the part itself.
  3. From the Guidance panel, select the X for that part.
