Add an image

An image is any graphic or picture that may help illustrate your meaning.

You must have an image ready to add and know its location.

Images can be a helpful way of showing rather than telling.

If your topic is non-rooted, only the NO IMAGE optional will be available in the IMAGE tab.

  1. In your topic, put your cursor where you want to place the image.
  2. From the toolbar, select Insert Image.
  3. Browse to your image.
    • If the image is on your local drive, choose Browse local.
    • If the image is on your server, choose Browse server.
    • If you have a non-rooted topic, choose No image and type or paste a path and file name.
  4. Select Adjust and set the properties of the image.
    • Placement:
      • In-line: For images that are part of a sentence.
      • Left: To left align an image. For images that are on their own line.
      • Center: To center the image on the page. For images that are on their own line.
      • Right: To right align an image. For images that are on their own line.
    • Scale:
      • Scale: The percent size of the original image. 50% makes the image half the size of the source image size.
      • Width and height: Dimensions of the image.
    • Alt text: Text that displays when the topic is published to HTML when someone hovers over the image and that a screen reader reads.
  5. Click OK.
Your image displays in the topic (unless your topic is non-rooted, in which case a small placeholder displays).
