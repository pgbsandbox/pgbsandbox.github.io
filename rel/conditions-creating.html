Creating a new condition

Only conditions that were found in the topic are shown in the sidebar. You can create and apply new ones to your topic.

When applying a condition to text, you can choose from conditions that already exist in your topic. You can also create new conditions if you want to.

  1. Open the conditions sidebar from the Toolbox.
  2. Click on APPLY CONDITIONS to reveal the panel which allows you to apply and remove conditions.
  3. Click on New Condition.
  4. Select a condition name from the drop down list.
  5. Type a value for this condition name in the box below.
    Tip: You may not include white space in the value of a condition.
  6. Click Add.

The new condition will be added to available choices under Select Conditions, and the new condition will have a color assigned to it in the legend below.
