Add Review Comments

A reviewer can add comments to the topic. The comments will not appear in the published output.

  1. Place your cursor in the text to which the comment will apply.
    A comment field is displayed in the right margin
  2. Type your comment in the gray area
    • Choose Save to add your comment, or
    • Choose Cancel to discard it
  3. To edit  or delete a comment, choose the appropriate option from the sidebar (hamburger) menu
  4. To reply to a comment, click in the text area of the comment and type your reply in the gray area. 
    • Choose Reply to add your comment
    • Choose Cancel to discard it.
