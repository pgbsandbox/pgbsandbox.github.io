Add a new part

Your topic consists of parts that you assemble to create your entire topic. You can see your parts, both the ones you have and the ones you may need, in your Guidance panel. You can add as many parts as you need for your content.

You add parts to the authoring pane by selecting the parts offered by the guidance panel that have not yet been used. You cannot add parts to the guidance panel.

The parts that are available differ depending on which topic type you are writing with. The following instructions are specific to a task topic, but the general idea is the same for all topic types.

In this task, we are going to add a BEFORE YOU BEGIN part to a task topic.

  1. From the Guidance panel, select BEFORE YOU BEGIN.
    Note: There is hover help for each part in the Guidance panel. If you are not sure which part to add, hover over the ?.
    If you do not already have a BEFORE YOU BEGIN part in your topic, BEFORE YOU BEGIN is listed with a plus. You can only add one of these parts.
    The new part with some hint text is added to your topic. That part in Guidance panel now has a check mark.
  2. In the authoring area, write content in the new part that you added.
