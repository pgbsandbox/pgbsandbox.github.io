Add a new heading

When you are working in topics other than task, you may want to add a subheading to separate your sections.

You can only add one level of headings in any topic. Remember that topics are intended to be short and focussed.

The heading button works like a toggle, allowing you to both apply and remove the formatting for a heading.

  1. In your topic, press Enter to put your cursor on a new, blank line.
    If you are in a list, de-select the list icon from the toolbar.
  2. Type your new heading.
  3. From the toolbar, select Section Heading.
    Your text becomes a heading and displays in the Guidance panel.
  4. Press Enter and add text, a list, a graphic, or a table after your new heading.
