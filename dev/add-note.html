Add a note

Notes call out small parts of information to bring them to the attention of the reader.

There are many types of notes you can add. They can contain a little or a lot of information.

  1. In your topic, place your cursor where you want to insert your note.
  2. From the toolbar, select Note.
    A note with no specific type is inserted.
  3. Select your note type.
  4. Add your note content.
    You can add multiple paragraphs to your note, including lists.
  5. To end your note, create a blank line at the end of the note and then press Enter or use the down arrow key.
  6. To split your note into two notes, and optionally add content between them, create a blank line in the place you want to split it and press Enter.
