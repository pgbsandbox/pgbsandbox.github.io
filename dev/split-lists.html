Split a list into two lists

You can easily split lists into two when needed.

Sometimes, you need to reorganize lists or explain a group of items differently from another group. In that case, you have one list that should be split into two different lists.

Enter on a blank list item ends the list if you are on the last list item; otherwise, it splits the list in two.

  1. Place your cursor at the beginning of the list item that will become the first item in a new list.
  2. Press Enter.
    A new, empty list item is created.
  3. Press Up arrow to move into the empty list item.
  4. Press Enter again.
The empty list item disappears and the single list is now two lists.
You can add text or a heading between the two lists.
